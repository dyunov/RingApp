How to add a ring

  • (Under construction: on the utilities page, fill out the "check for duplicates" form and see if your ring is in the results.)
  • On the admin home page, click the "add" option in the row for Ring.
  • Fill in as much information for the ring as you can (use the keyword selector and ignore kwd.)
  • Hit "save." Your ring is now in the database.
  • Hopefully you had some key properties of the ring in mind. You ought to follow the following instructions to add them.
  • Return to the admin home page. Click "add" in the "Ring-property relationships" row, or the commutative counterpart.
  • Select your ring in the ring selector (it is probably at the bottom.)
  • Select a property you are interested in from the drop-down.
  • In "has property," put a 1 if the ring HAS the property, and a 0 if it does NOT have the property.
  • Use the "reason" field if the reason is something very straightforward. Use the "source" field to include a citation if you can.
  • If the form demands content in one of the text fields, you can skirt it by submitting whitespace.
  • Click "save" or "save and add another" depending on if you are done or not.
  • Remember that you don't have to supply all properties you know about the ring, just "the best" ones.
  • (Under construction: on the utilities page, select your ring from the dropdown and hit "run processor")
